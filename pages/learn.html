Explore about Plastic Pollution

Impact of Plastic Pollution in different Earth spheres


Did you know?
The world generates 400 million tonnes of plastic waste a year.

Geosphere

  • Degrades soil quality
  • Causes problems for land animals who accidentally swallowed or inhaled plastic pollutants
  • Non-biodegradable plastic items fill up the landfills.
  • Plastic that are chlorinated can release harmful chemicals into the surrounding soil, which impacts the fertility of the soil.
  • Plastic particles has led to small living organisms on the geosphere to die out.


Hydrosphere

  • By 2050, plastic will likely outweigh all fish in the sea.
  • Plastic waste makes up 80% of all marine pollution.
  • Around 700 species of marine animals were affected by the microplastics in the ocean.
  • A study show that nearly all aquatic organisms ingest microplastics.


Atmosphere

  • Microplastics and nanoplastics have been found in the air particles according to The Center for International Environmental Law.
  • This means we're literally breathing plastic as we live.
  • It‚Äôs estimated that humans can inhale up to 22,000,000 micro- and nanoplastics every year.
  • Such tiny plastics can harm our human body, including the lungs.


Biosphere

  • In summary, all living organisms and the entire world is affected by plastic pollution, with only the variation of how much each are affected.
