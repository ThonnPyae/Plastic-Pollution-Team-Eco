How we can use this website

By utilizing this website, we can learn how plastic impacts the earth in different degrees, the problems or controversies shrouding plastic pollution, alternatives to using plastic while showing their daily usages, actions that people, countries and organizations are taking and the measures we have taken to make learning about plastic pollution via games easy and efficient too.

Our Story

TeamEco is a group built from students that attend EDUSN International Digital School hoping to spread awareness to others about environmental problems such as plastic pollution. With strong hearts and undetering willpower, we aspire to achieve a better future for all ages.

Our Mission

As students of the EDUSN International Digital School and developers of TeamEco, our group's aim is to encourage youths and adults whether locally or internationally to take action against plastic pollution by enlightening them with our prior knowledge and researched information that can found in our website while creating easy to understand designs and activities to participate in which can help them regulate plastic waste and reduce plastic pollution globally.
